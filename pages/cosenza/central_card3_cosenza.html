Landscapes

Inmerse yourself in nature

The journey continues to explore the most spectacular natural landscapes!
Between the sea and the mountains, this city has it all! Discover local traditions, from the sandwich with 'salsiccia' and 'caciocavallo' in Camigliatello to 'cuccia' in Pedace, from the 'pomodori' in Belmonte to 'cuddrurieddri' in Cosenza.
Each season has its own typical dish, you will love them!



Likewise, you can choose from a variety of outdoor activities. If you enjoy snow, you can head to the mountains for a great ski session or a moonlit walk.
If you love the sea, what are you waiting for? Take the plunge! And don't forget to catch the sunset on the beach with friends!
Explore the most beautiful landscapes walking, by bike, or why not, on a motorcycle! Beautiful trails to discover breathtaking views await you!
