The journey continues to explore the most spectacular sights!
Climb the tallest skyscrapers and look down on the city, among the most beautiful are the Empire State Building, the Summit and Edge. the city seen from above is magnificent!

St Patrick church is very impressive, in front of the Rockefeller Center, or the walk on the Brooklyn bridge as well as the beaches of Coney Island. Do not miss Time Square at night, set of many movies, it will dazzle you with its night lights.
