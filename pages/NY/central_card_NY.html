New York is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. Central Park is the biggest green area of the city and maybe the best starting point for the tour. Many outdoor activities are done here: walking, running, and bicicling. A good suggestion is to find a beautiful accomodation near manatthan such as this one.
The transfers from the airports are simple and if you find an accomodation close to a subway stop is better for walk around autonomusly.



Subways are safe and efficient in the Manhattan area. There are different types of stops, not every train stops each of these. Look carrefully the direction, google maps works fine to help you.
If you wanna a better overview about how the subway works, you can read this guide. In the same guide you can find different solution to reach the city center from the airports.

Every subway ride costs around 2.90$, but if you use the OMNY app, linking your credit/debit card, you will pay a maximum of 34$ each week. You pay just the first 12 rides, afther these the others will be free.
After registration on the website you can use the card connected to the entrance of the metro stop. Also works on buses.
