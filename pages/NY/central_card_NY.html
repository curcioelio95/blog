Discover this awesome city!

Cosenza is a small town in Calabria, Southern Italy, situated in the heart of the region. It's approximately half an hour away from both the sea and the mountains. Small villages surround the city, stretching from the coastline to the mountains, each brimming with traditions and fascinating stories. If you're seeking a peaceful place steeped in history to explore, read on; you've come to the right destination!



The city boasts marvelous treasures! Overlooking the city is a stunning medieval castle that can be visited and serves also as an active venue for city events.
The city has a very ancient history, it's also the birthplace of the mystery surrounding King Alaric and his treasure, both buried at the confluence of the Crati and Busento rivers.



Begin your walk through the streets of the city, starting from Piazza Fera (also known as Piazza Bilotti) and continuing all the way to the town hall, where you can admire the old town! Keep climbing up the hill, exploring the narrow streets of the old town.
As you walk, take some time to visit the cathedral; it will be worth it! Once at the top, you can admire the beautiful Rendano Theater, immersing yourself in the greenery of 'Villa Vecchia'!
